Our Product

Green Office produces recycled pencil cases with a solar-powered phone charger. The pencil case is 8.25” x 2.75”, and has a solar panel charger of 7” x 2”. The case is made of recycled plastic to promote sustainability. For vanity reasons, the solar panel has a cover to hide it when not in use and pencil cases come in a variety of colors. Per each charge, phone batteries have an average “talk time” of 5-7 hours, which is less than a typical school or work day. Therefore, including a solar-powered charger will help if one’s phone battery dies and there is no access to an outlet.
